--- title: "Badger: File format validation for CI/CD and scripting" h1: Badger --- Badger is a tool for validating that all your files are in the correct format. ## Fancy parameters * Range - accepts a value within a range * `MIN-MAX`: anything between MIN and MAX (inclusive) * `MIN-`: at least MIN * `-MAX`: at most MAX * `VALUE`: exactly VALUE (i.e. MIN and MAX are the same) * DecimalRange - similar to `Range`, but the values can be decimals * Ratio - the ratio of two numbers * `X:Y`
